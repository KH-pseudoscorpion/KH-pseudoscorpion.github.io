Ph.D. Student  ·  Leibniz Institute for the Analysis of Biodiversity Change, University of Hamburg

I study the phylogeography and diversity of pseudoscorpions across East Asia, investigating how the region's complex geological history has shaped the dispersal and evolutionary history of these small but ecologically significant arachnids. My interests extend to the broader distribution and evolutionary history of various invertebrates.
